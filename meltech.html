Introduction

Doctor Mel does not descriminate anybody based on ethnic origin , social status , age , nationality , gender nor sexual orientation. As a company and as an individual , Doctor Mel is commited and dedicated not only to the service of the health of Mexicans , but to that of individuals living in Mexican Territory regardless of their nationality in the event of them not being Mexican Nationals. Because of this , as a company and as an individual , Doctor Mel takes an impartial political position and lets the immigration professionals to take care of such issues and instead takes a Hipocratic position: " to save and take care of human lives" , and because of that as a company and as an individual , Doctor Mel positions himself within the frame of human service exclusively , irrespective of legal externalities such as nationality and political affiliation. In addition , Doctor Mel is commited and dedicated also to the social of progress of Mexico.

In excercising a commitment to not excercise descrimitation , Doctor Mel takes into account one lone thing to authorize a professional in his ranks : to fulfill the requirements , if a candidate is a gay man , 70 years old , Masonic for example , that is irrelevant to Doctor Mel , we have strict standards toward doctors such as not engaging in conversations that deal with politics , that express dejection or derogation toward homosexuals , that express denigration towards those in a challenging immigration status in our country when they are on duty.

Doctor Mel takes his Medicine labor very seriously as a company and as an individual and because of that I demand the highest standards possible. For the only way in which we as Doctors can excercise our labor continuously , is by giving and demanding the best to and from each other.

Our Mission

In fulfilling our Hipocratic Oath , anyone working for me must make the health of humans and their wellbeing a number one priority. Because of that we must set aside our religious and political views when on duty , we have patients from all walks of life , religions , political affiliations , gender identity and sexual preferences , I will not tolerate any bias nor prejudice in favor nor against any individual coming from any of my doctors to any of my patients , so if you have set religious beliefs and political ideologies that do not tolerate difference of points of view and belief to yours , and/or if you harbor prejudice against non-heterosexual preferences , please do not apply to my firm.

List of requirements

As mentioned earlier , the minimum level of education you must have in order to be considered for an entry level position is being in your last year of College.

In addition to that you must show up on time , it goes without a saying that the further away you live the more difficult this will be , bus lines do not run to this part of town , so reliable trasportation must be a requirement from our part to ensure you can be on time.

The applying process

We ask you to send your resume to doctormel@doctormel.com where I will personally review each candidate , it is my intention to give a fair chance to everybody and I do my best to do so.

Once I have selected candidates I feel are a good for our Medical firm , I go ahead and contact that person , it takes me no more than 3 days to make a selection.

The selection process

Once I have contacted a candidate I have them come to my office for an interview , over a cup of coffee or tea we exchange points of view to determine if we both are a good fit for the other , time is precious and I schedule my interviews all on a row in one day.

At the end of that round I select the best fit and the next step is to work out a time to start that works for both parties.

Conclusion
