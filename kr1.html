The Emperor ... feel like a King or a Queen ...

The emperor of Hungary was a shrewd man.

His favorite drink was Zwack.

With this cocktail, we use vermouth and Grand Marnier to elevate Zwack to its full potential,
just like the Hungarian people did for their emperor... I'm guessing, I don't know. I'm a bartender,
not a historian, leave me alone , I can tell you this though; this cocktail is herbal, lightly bitter,
citrus sweet, and ooooooooohhhh so satisfying.

Some might say it's fit for royalty.

- Peter Vicklund

Gethsemane ... as close to The Heavenly Kingdom as it gets ...

Close your eyes...

Imagine you're standing in a doorway to a temple, facing a garden... sandalwood incense is burning behind you...
... the garden is full of flowers.

In the center is a lemon tree... over-ripe lemons that fell from the tree fill your nose with citrus ...
you feel at peace...
... now ...
... put this heavenly experience in a drink .

That's what you get when you order Gethsemane at The Kaiser's , you're welcome btw.

- Peter Vicklund

Nouveau ... the coolest raspberry in The Kaiser's Kingdom , period !

Can you imagine a raspberry playing a saxophone?

That's kind of what this cocktail tastes like.
The sharp tart of raspberry and lemon is rounded out with the dark, moody flavor of sloe gin.
The finish lingers with dry oak and juniper , this the old made new again.
Enjoy.

- Peter Vicklund

Trust me ... you'll like it ... Try it ... you'll like it

This is a lemon drop, but completely different.

If you're feeling adventurous, this is the cocktail for you.
If you don't like vodka, try this cocktail.

Just...try it. You'll like it.
