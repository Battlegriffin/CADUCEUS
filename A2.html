About Us

Who we are

Hello , my name is Kaiser Santosvictorious , bar manager of this pub and I am going to tell you a little bit about our concept and for that I have to tell you a little bit about the roots of our foundation .

Mediterranean , Germanic and peoples of the British Isles , in that order, were the first Europeans to have contact with India and as the saying goes , the rest is history.

Out of all , the Greeks and Romans were the first Europeans to have contact with India , Greek philosophers and Indian philosophers exchanged ideas and this Greco-Indian philosophy link is well documented.

The Romans under their first Emperor , Augustus Caesar were the
ones to introduce the magic of India to Europe , exposing them to
spices , textiles , mathematical advances , art from India .

Swords were invented by Indians from Damascus and due to the fact
that the Romans held Indians in great esteem and cultivated excellent
relationships with them , they were chosen as their biggest trading
European partners for centuries , this allowed the Romans to have
access to the many mathematical , engineering , architectural ,
chemical , metalurgical discoveries Indians made and this in turn
allowed the Romans for centuries to have an edge over all European
peoples , the Vikings , The Gauls , The Celts .

The first war sword is The Roman sword , which is a modification of the
Damascus sword , the rest of European peoples started making their
own sword but for a long time nobody could compete with Roman swords , their secret : Indian swordsmiths .

Romans took Indian swordsmiths to live at their capital and make
swords to their specifiations , the secret of Indian sowrdsmiths ? :
Damascus steel .

Indians from Damascus were the first recorded people to discovered
how to process steel into usable tools and weapons and did so long
before Europeans did .

For a long time no European people was able to tell that Roman swords
were made of steel , and specifically , Damascus steel , the combination
of Damascus steel with the mathematical , chemical and engineering
skill of Swrodsmiths from Damascus gave Rome weaponry advantage for a longtime , as the rest of Europeans made their swords with iron ,
an inflexible and easily corruptible metal and they thought that
Romans were simply making iron take a certain texture by some
process , consequently they spent in their ignorance a long time
treating iron to be "Roman iron" , this was of course , impossible
and Romans laughed at the rest of Europeans for this for a long time .

Once Germanic , Scandinavian and British peoples learned about
the Roman secret , India , they started expeditions of their own there ,
but for a long time Rome and all Mediterranean Europeans enjoyed the favor of Indians and their knowledge of chemistry , mathematics , spices and metalurgy went to them until England conquered India and appropriated to himself all that .

Many alcoholic drinks we consume nowadays started as elixirs to cure maladies and here is where India plays a very important role in the culture of every single European group that existed and exists to this day , for without India , the alcoholic drinks that European Kings , Queens and peasants enjoyed would never had existed , why ? , take a look:

Indian Licorice , used for centuries in Germany to make digestifs of which the most popular modern day descendant is Jagermaiser, pomegranate , used for centuries in Eastern Europe to make one of their traditional spirits : pomegranate wine , star anice , used for centuries in Italy for the making of liqueur Galliano , star anise has been used for centuries as well by the Germanic peoples of modern day Switzerland to make our beloved ... absinthe ! , just in these examples you can see that some of the most European of European liqueurs and digestifs are possible thanks to India , India even made a realtively recent contribution to the existence of Navy Strength gin and Indian Pale Ale beer by having British sailors and businessmen alter the consistency of their gin and beer in India when they colonized it .

India has , since Europeans have been able to travel , made an impact in to the most vital aspects of the life European peoples , their food , their drinking , their mathematics , weaponry and architectural knowledge , and at Kaiser' Elixir Room we focus on bringing to you original recipies
of Indo-European origin , we also include in our menu delicious cocktails
made with spirits and ingredients from The Americas , thus bringing to you a truly intenational cocktail experience with Indo-European roots .

We toast for you to enjoy our Indo-European recipies and Pub as much as we have enjoyed bringing these elixirs and Pub to life .
